About JR

How I Found My Spiritual Path!

Welcome, and Merry Meet. My name is JR. Hasting and I am a solitary practitioner living in the upstate of South Carolina. Like most pagans I am a free spirit that was once trapped in the not so perfect world of mainstream religion. I thought and really truly believed that what other people found spiritually satisfying was what I had to except as my own. It was during my young teenage years that I decided to leave the beliefs of my parents and family behind and seek spirituality for myself. It did not take very long for me to find the religion of Wicca. Similar to most people in the late 2000’s I was mourning the loss of the television show Charmed and it was during a rerun of episode one that noticed Phoebe had mentioned “Wicca” and I just had to know more from there.
...
I began searching for all the information I could find at the middle school I was attending just to be lead down a blind path that had me more confused than I was before and it was during that moment that the doubts of converting religions became a reality for me. Lucky for me, a few years later my aunt and I were having a conversation and the religion I was once so intrigued by was brought up. She had a friend who was a practicing Witch and was dabbling a bit in the practice herself for a while. It was through her that I was pointed in the direction I needed to be to achieve what I was missing mentally, emotionally, and spiritually. I began my practice reading books by authors including Scott Cunningham, David Buckland, Christopher Penczak, Silver Ravenwolf, Thea Sabin, as well as many others. I was completely fascinated with everything I was learning for years before I began to practice the Craft myself. It was 2011, two years later, that I first took on the term “Wiccan” as my own.
...
Now, fast forward to 2018 I am still a Witch but I do not associate myself as Wiccan but rather as a Solitary Eclectic Practitioner. I have chosen this path because it fits my spirituality like white on rice. I am able now to live the life that I love with a belief system I hold dear because I choose what I find that rings true with me the most as a spiritual being and go with it. If you would like more information about this path that I am currently on in life then check out my blog and explore this website!
